▸ Spinal Anatomy Guide

CERVICAL (C1–C7): Neck region. Supports the skull. Most mobile section. Vulnerable to flexion injury. Safe ROM ±45°.

THORACIC (T1–T12): Mid-back, articulates with ribs. Kyphotic curve. More rigid. Safe ROM ±35°.

LUMBAR (L1–L5): Lower back, bears greatest load. Lordotic curve. Most common disc herniation site. Safe ROM ±25°.

SACRUM: Fused vertebrae connecting spine to pelvis. Weight distribution base.

▸ Posture Tips
✓ Ears aligned over shoulders over hips
✓ Screen at eye level, arm's length away
✓ Feet flat, knees at 90° when seated
⚠ Forward head = +2.5 kg load per 2.5 cm
⚠ Posture break every 30 minutes
✗ No sustained bend >20° for >10 min
✗ Asymmetric loading damages discs over time
